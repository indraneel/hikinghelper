About Hiking Helper

Hiking Helper wants to make outdoor activities more accessible for everyone. Even if you enjoy spending time in nature, if you aren't familiar with the activities in your area, planning a trip can be a hassle. There are lots of resources online to help you learn more, but that takes more time than you have and it's hard to pick from all the options. Right now Hiking Helper is just a team of one, but if you tell me a little about yourself and the kinds of activities you enjoy, I'll help you create a custom itinerary for your weekend.
